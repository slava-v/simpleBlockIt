Enter list of urls to be blocked

Synthax used is same as here https://developer.chrome.com/extensions/match_patterns.html

Urls list: (Each line, new rule. Empty lines, and lines starting with ";" are ignored)

Expressions

Expression Rules

<url-pattern> := <scheme>://<host><path>
<scheme> := '*' | 'http' | 'https' | 'file' | 'ftp' | 'chrome-extension'
<host> := '*' | '*.' <any char except '/' and '*'>+
<path> := '/' <any chars>
